About Me


Hi! My name is Lauren Romanelli. I am a rising junior at James Madison University, a beautiful school tucked away in the mountains of the Shenandoah Valley. I am a double major in Communication Studies and Media Arts & Design. Upon graduation I plan to work in either UX design or digital media production.
